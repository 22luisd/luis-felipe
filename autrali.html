Austria

Australia is a continental country surrounded by the Indian and Pacific Oceans. Its main cities, Sydney, Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth and Adelaide, are coastal. Its capital, Canberra, is not coastal. The country is known for the Sydney Opera House, the Great Barrier Reef, the vast inland desert called the Outback, and unique animal species such as kangaroos and platypuses.
