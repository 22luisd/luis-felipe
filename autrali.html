Austria

Australia is a country located in Oceania. Geographically, Australia is an island territory with a great diversity of fauna and flora, precisely as a result of its geographic isolation. Australian territory was colonized by the English, which strongly influenced the country's current culture. The local native population, the Aborigines, also plays an important cultural role in Australian society. Australia is a very developed and industrialized nation, which is why it is one of the countries with the best quality of life in the world.
